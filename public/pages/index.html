Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I list my car for sale?

Click on the "Sell Your Car" link in the navigation menu, fill out the required details, and upload photos of your car.

2. What types of cars can I find on CarConnect?

CarConnect features both old and new cars from various brands and models to suit your preferences.

3. Is there a fee for listing a car?

Currently, listing your car is free of charge.

4. How do I contact a seller?

You can find the seller's contact information on the car's details page.

5. Can I schedule a test drive?

Yes, you can coordinate directly with the seller to schedule a test drive.

6. What payment methods are accepted?

Payment terms are agreed upon between the buyer and seller. CarConnect does not handle payments.

7. How do I report a fraudulent listing?

If you suspect a fraudulent listing, please contact us at support@carconnect.com.
